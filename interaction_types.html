Types of Species Interactions

Introduction

Species interactions play a vital role in shaping ecosystems and maintaining biodiversity. They describe the effects species have on each other's fitness, which can be positive (+), negative (-), or neutral (/). Below, we explore different types of interactions along with their ecological implications.
