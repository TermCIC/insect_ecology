Statistic Quiz

Imagine two crypto investors, Alex and Jamie, who earn money by buying and selling Bitcoin. You want to determine who is the better investor by analyzing their performance data. Each investor's profits (\(y\)) depend on general market profits (\(x\)). Use linear regression to find their regression formula and compare their intercepts (\(b\)) to determine who performs better under poor market conditions (\(x \approx 0\)).

Task

Below are the randomly generated datasets for Alex and Jamie. Perform the regression analysis using your preferred tool and answer:

  • What is the regression formula for each investor?
  • Who has the higher intercept (\(b\))?
  • Which investor performs better in poor market conditions?
  • Which investor performs better than the general market profits? (Test if their regression slope (\(m\)) is significantly different from 1 using a t-test).
